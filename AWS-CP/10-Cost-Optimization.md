# Cost Optimization

## Pricing models
- On-Demand: flexible.
- Reserved Instances/Savings Plans: predictable workloads.
- Spot: fault-tolerant workloads.

## Cost tools
- Cost Explorer, Budgets, Cost & Usage Report.
- Trusted Advisor checks.

## Common savings
- Turn off idle resources.
- Use S3 lifecycle policies.
- Right-size EC2 and RDS.
